Protecting Environment for Future Generations

The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) is a statutory organization under the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change. It promotes cleanliness of streams and wells, improves air quality, and ensures sustainable environmental practices across India.

Air quality monitoring stations track pollutants and ensure safer air standards.
Water treatment plants help reduce contamination and protect aquatic life.
Industries are regulated to manage hazardous waste effectively.
Noise pollution guidelines ensure peaceful living environments.
Afforestation programs help restore ecological balance.
